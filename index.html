What are good wines made of?

Although the quality of wine is a subjective question and partially a matter of taste, many good wines still share similar characteristics. Cortez et al (see data source) have created a dataset listing various chemical properties of Portuguese Vinho Verde wines, along with subjective expert evaluations on their quality on a scale from 1 to 10. This chart demonstrates some of the relationships between chemical properties of red wines and their perceived quality.

Data source:

P. Cortez, A. Cerdeira, F. Almeida, T. Matos and J. Reis. Modeling wine preferences by data mining from physicochemical properties. In Decision Support Systems, Elsevier, 47(4):547-553. ISSN: 0167-9236.
